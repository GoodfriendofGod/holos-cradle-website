What does 'Holos Cradle' mean?

"Holos" is Greek for "whole." A "Cradle" is a safe place where new life begins. Together, Holos Cradle is the safe, foundational place where the journey to wholeness begins.

Our culture often misunderstands the multi-talented person. We are told to pick one lane, and so we end up feeling scattered—a collection of talents without a singular, powerful purpose. I know this because it was my story.

This sense of fragmentation doesn't just affect us as individuals; it's the same hidden disease that causes organizations to fail—their work separated from their purpose, their skills from their spirit. They may achieve measures of success, but they are not whole.

I have made it my life's mission to solve this. As The Integrationist, I provide the principles and tools to help ambitious individuals forge their diverse skills into a unified strength, and to build organizations where every part works in perfect harmony.
